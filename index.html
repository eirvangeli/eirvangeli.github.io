Bio

Hi, I'm Eirini, originally from Greece, now based in Los Angeles, and endlessly inspired by the space between cultures. I'm a visual storyteller at heart: someone who sees a story in everything and instinctively thinks about how to share it. I love creating content that feels intentional and alive, the kind that makes people stop scrolling because something about it actually resonates.

As an advancement coordinator (marketing & development) with Philadelphia Youth Choral Ensembles, I channeled that instinct into something I'm really proud of: annual tour journals and merchandise, each one designed around the country the ensemble was visiting that year. Built from scratch, rooted in culture, and made to be kept. I come at design from a marketing perspective and every visual choice I make starts with what the audience needs to feel.
